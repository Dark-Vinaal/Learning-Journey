## 🚘 Driver Commands

### 📌 `driverquery`

#### Description:

- Lists installed drivers.
```cmd
driverquery
```

#### Where & When Used:

- Driver diagnostics

---

### 📌 `driverquery | findstr <keyword>`

#### Description:

- Filters driver list.
```cmd
driverquery | findstr wifi
```

#### Where & When Used:

- Locate specific drivers

---
