## 🔋 Power & Battery Commands

### 📌 `powercfg /energy`

#### Description:

- Analyzes system power efficiency.
```cmd
powercfg /energy
```

#### Where & When Used:

- Power optimization
- Laptop diagnostics

---

### 📌 `powercfg /batteryreport`

#### Description:

- Generates battery health report.
```cmd
powercfg /batteryreport
```

#### Where & When Used:

- Battery health monitoring

---
